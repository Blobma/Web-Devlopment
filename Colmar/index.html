Classic Dining Options

This city is known for inventing some of the classical french/italian dishes such the very famous foie gras, pain d'epices, saeurkraut, le coque au riesling, baecoffe and tarte flambee are just some of the ancient dishes that are traditionally/occassionally prepared in this village.
